Strategic innovations

Where PAMOJA is building its next layer of capability

Five structural bets the programme is making in 2026 — moving from project to system, from story to narrative, from campaign to ecosystem.

NED — Narrative Ecosystem Design

New SBC paradigm replacing the Sabido-era 360° campaign. From one story to an orchestrated narrative cloud — RAES designs, runs and governs interconnected actors, stories, platforms and social dynamics to stabilize narratives that shift norms and behaviours. Tested in Côte d'Ivoire and Benin, scaling across the portfolio in 2026.

LAFAAAC

RAES training catalogue delivered via white-label mobile app + LMS (Teach on Mars / Lumapps). Builds capacity for internal teams, CSO partners and creative actors on SDGs, sexual & reproductive health and the Cultural & Creative Industries.

Digital studio

In-house production capability for rapid-response creative, opposition monitoring content and platform-native storytelling across the 7-country portfolio.

Opposition videos

Monitoring, analysing and responding to opposition narratives in near-real time — defensive and offensive posture to protect the programme's narrative gains.

Regional campaign design

Cross-country narrative architecture around the draft African Charter on the Protection of the Family — PAMOJA's first regional campaign.
